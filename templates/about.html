About me

My name is Drobota Andrei, and I'm thrilled to share a bit about my journey with you. In 2017, I proudly graduated from high school, equipped with a strong foundation of knowledge and a passion for learning. Eager to delve deeper into the world of engineering, I embarked on a new chapter of my academic journey.

From 2017 to 2021, I dedicated myself to pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in HVAC systems. During this transformative period, I immersed myself in the intricacies of mechanical design, thermodynamics, and HVAC technology, gaining valuable insights and skills that would shape my future endeavors.

In 2022, fueled by a desire for new experiences and opportunities, I made the bold decision to relocate to Germany. This move marked the beginning of an exciting chapter filled with cultural immersion and personal growth.

In 2023, I took a significant leap into the world of software development by enrolling in the Diploma in Full Stack Software Developer program at Code Institute. Embracing the challenge with enthusiasm, I eagerly delved into the realms of coding, web development, and software engineering, driven by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a passion for innovation.
